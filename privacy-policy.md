# Privacy Policy

**IsQRSecure**
Last updated: March 2026

## Overview

IsQRSecure is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data stays on your device.

## Camera Access

The app requests camera access solely to scan QR codes. Camera images are processed on-device in real time and are never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

## Scan History

Your scan history (URLs, timestamps, and quiz answers) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device. You can delete individual entries or clear all history at any time from the History tab.

## No Analytics or Tracking

IsQRSecure does not use any analytics frameworks, tracking pixels, advertising identifiers, or third-party services. We do not track how you use the app.

## No Network Requests

The app does not make any network requests. It works entirely offline. The only network activity occurs if you choose to open a scanned URL in your browser, which is handled by iOS Safari, not by this app.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. There is no data to share because we do not collect any.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children. The app is educational in nature and suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected in an app update. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised accordingly.
